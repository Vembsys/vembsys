About Vembsys Technovation

Founded in 2006, Vembsys Technovation Pvt. Ltd. is a pioneering company dedicated to advancing automotive technology. Under the leadership of our esteemed director, Dr. D S Khatri, who holds a PhD in Engine Management Systems from IIT Delhi, we have established ourselves as a leader in the field.

Our commitment to research and development is reflected in the 43 research papers we have published in both national and international conferences and journals. Our work has been recognized globally, with several of our papers receiving prestigious best paper awards.

At Vembsys Technovation, we combine expertise and innovation to drive the future of automotive technology, making significant contributions to the industry both in production and research.

Core Competencies

Our expertise is in Research and Development (R&D), with a focus on developing application and embedded software for the automobile industry. This includes working on advanced engine management systems and innovative technologies aimed at improving vehicle performance and compliance with stringent emission standards like BS-6.

The company excels in emission-controlled technologies, advanced battery management systems, motor controllers for electric vehicles, and electronic control units (ECUs). We provide solutions that meet stringent standards, enhance vehicle efficiency, and ensure regulatory compliance.

The company excels in enhancing engine performance, developing alternative energy systems, designing ECUs, and specializing in embedded systems. It also implements emission control and after-treatment technologies for environmental compliance.
